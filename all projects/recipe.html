Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1cup unsalted butter,melted
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 tea spoons vanilla extracts
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon bakingsoda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semisweets choclate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the ovan to 350 degree F (175 degree C )
  2. In a large bowl,mix together the melted butter ,white sugar,and brown sugar until well blended
  3. Beat in the vanilla extract eggs untillight and creamy.
  4. combine the flour bakingsoda baking powder and salt in a seprate bowl gradually stir into the butter and sugar mixture
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips by hand usinga wooden spoon
  6. Drop cookie dough by rounded spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets
  7. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the preheated oven or untill edges are lightly browned
  8. Cool on baking sheets for a few minutes before transfering to wire racks to cool completely

Notes

For softer cookies reduce baking time slightly, for crisper cookies increase baking time slightly, enjoy your homemade chocolate chips cookies witha glass of milk!
