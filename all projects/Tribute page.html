swami vivekananda

swami vivekananda ,a keyfigure in the introduction of indian philosphies of vedanta and yoga to the western world , played a crucial role in the revival of hinduismin india his inspiratinal speeches and teachings continue to influence and inspire people around the globe

About the legend

  • Swami vivekananda was born as narendra datta on january 12 ,1863,in kolkatta,india
  • he was brilliant student and had a keen intrest in spirtuality from a young age
  • vivekananda become a disciple of saint ramakrishna ,who taught him that all living being were an embodiment of the divine self
  • he is best known for his speech at the parliament of the world religions in chicago in 1893 , wherehe began his speech with "sisters and brothers of america"
  • vivekananda found the ramakrishna mission in 1897 ,which works in the areas of education,healthcare, and social service
  • His teaching focused in the potiential divinty of every individual and the idea of serving humanity as a wayto serve god
  • swami vivekananda books on the four yogas are highly regarded and widely read
  • he passed away on july 4,1902,at the young age of 39 ,but his legacy continues to live on through his teachings and the institutions he established.
